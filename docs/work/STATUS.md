## How initiatives are classified

- **Active** — has tasks in flight (any `T-*.md` with `status: in-progress`, or a mix of done + pending).
- **Backlog** — no `T-*.md` yet (not atomized), or all tasks `pending`.
- **Done** — every `T-*.md` is `done` or `obsolete`, with at least one `done`. Also when `plan.md` frontmatter has `status: done`.
- **Obsoleted** — `plan.md` frontmatter has `status: obsolete`, or every `T-*.md` is `obsolete`.
